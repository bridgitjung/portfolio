I am a UX/Product designer who strives to learn and create.

Being a girl toying with graphic design and designing my Tumblr blog theme (which this portfolio is inspired by), I have grown from that little designer to create informed solutions to modern problems in today's world.

Now, I am grateful to be able to work with so many people of different backgrounds and skillsets towards a common goal: empowering users. My work focuses on social impact and accessibility.

I completed my Masters of Science in Information, focusing on UX Research and Design, at the University of Michigan School of Information in May 2026, and my Bachelors of Science in Information (UX Design path) with a Minor in Art & Design at the University of Michigan in May 2025.

Contact me at bridgit@umich.edu!
